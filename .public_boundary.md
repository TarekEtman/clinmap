# Public Boundary Rules

This repository is designed as a neutral public proof-of-work system.

Public-facing materials must not mention target platforms or hiring campaigns. They should describe the work as clinical AI evaluation engineering: synthetic cases, rubrics, response ranking, safety review, evaluator calibration, and reproducible scoring.

Rules:
- Use only synthetic examples.
- Do not include patient data, employer data, client data, platform task content, screenshots, proprietary prompts, proprietary rubrics, or confidential materials.
- Do not present content as medical advice or diagnosis.
- Do not overclaim physician authority.
- Do not describe the repository as built for a specific company.
