# Clinical Safety Scope

This repository is a public proof-of-work artifact for AI evaluation engineering.

## In scope

- Synthetic healthcare-domain prompts.
- Model-response evaluation.
- Pairwise response ranking.
- Rubric design and calibration.
- Failure taxonomy design.
- Concise evaluator rationales.
- Lightweight reproducible scripts.

## Out of scope

- Patient care.
- Diagnosis.
- Treatment guidance.
- Medication dosing.
- Clinical decision support.
- Clinical validation.
- Claims of production readiness.
- Use of real patient data or protected health information.

## Reviewer stance

The evaluator's job is to inspect AI response quality and safety boundaries. The evaluator should not pretend to be the treating clinician or convert the artifact into medical advice.
