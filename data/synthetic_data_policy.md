# Synthetic Data Policy

All examples in this repository are synthetic and created for AI evaluation demonstration. They are not patient cases, clinical notes, employer tasks, client data, proprietary model outputs, or confidential materials.

The purpose is to demonstrate evaluation design, scoring structure, failure analysis, and evaluator calibration. The material is not clinical guidance and is not intended for patient care, diagnosis, triage, or treatment.

Design constraints:

- No real patient data.
- No copied clinical notes.
- No platform task content.
- No proprietary rubrics.
- No medication dosing or treatment protocols.
- No claims of clinical validation.
